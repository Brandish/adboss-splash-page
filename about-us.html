ABOUT US

Brandish was imagined to be a completely different and unique experience for viewing and discovering the best products on the web. To make sure of that we asked a lot of questions:

What if you could self-select your own experience based on your interests?

How can we eliminate intrusive advertising?

What if brands could “push” their latest products and advances directly to you in a non-intrusive way?

How do you take the explosion of content that is available today and make it a very personal and complete experience for each individual user?

How do we create a one stop destination for brands and consumers to unite in a personal, interactive way that has never been seen in the mobile space?

How do we build a community of users that share their wants and haves in a fun and interesting way?

And finally….

How can we make it easier to discover, compare, and buy the products you want and already have everyday?

The answer, we found, is a combination of technology and old fashioned, hands-on curation that has become the Brandish Mobile Experience.

SELF SELECTION IS THE KEY

We realized advertising has been making great and compelling stories for over 90 years. The problem is, in the digital space, it always seems to interrupt what your doing. No matter how great an ad is, you probably don’t want it intruding into your time when you are looking for something specific on the web. But what if you could simply select your favorite products based on your interest? Instead of those products intruding on your time, you are able to view them when it’s convenient for you. Sounds great huh? Well it is! Brandish offers 23 Categories and over 300 sub-categories that span the interests of almost everyone! You select categories based on your interests and the latest and most relevant videos will be pushed directly to your personal feed. You can watch it on your time and always stay up to date on the latest and greatest products on the market today!

WE WATCH EVERYTHING

We don’t rely on technology to provide our users with the best content experience, we roll up our sleeves and do it ourselves. Our team of in-house curators watch every single video that you find on the Brandish app creating a unique and one-of-a-kind experience for you. We strive to make your experience as complete and personal as we can.

WANT IT? HAVE IT?

“Want it? Have it!” is a feature that brings together all your friends in the Brandish community. It’s social sharing, but kicked up a notch. See a product you want? Tag it! See a product you have? Tag it. Share these wants and haves with other user and begin conversations based on your interest. What could be better? Share you wants and haves with your friends! It’s easy and fun!

MILLIONS OF PRODUCTS, ONE APP

The Brandish app has been designed to be that ONE stop destination for all your product needs. It’s easy to use, interactive and quite frankly, addictive! The team at Brandish finds all the best brands and videos on the web, categorizes and presents them to you providing the most complete and relevant experience. Why waste time searching all over the web? We do the work, so you can sit back and enjoy! So that’s a quick overview of the thought that we’ve put into the Brandish mobile experience. We hope you find it to be the most personal, exciting and complete way to interact with today’s best brands and products!
