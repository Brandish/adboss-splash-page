THE BRANDISH EXPERIENCE

Let’s go over a few of our great features that will enhance your Brandish viewing experience: Feed, Want it? Have it! and Explore:

The “Feed” will create a truly custom and unique user experience. Simply select the “plus” icon found next to any video you like and this will add that category to your personal feed. Everyday you will receive the most relevant videos in your favorite selected categories. Get updates, new product releases and the latest content from only the categories you selected! Brandish makes sure you’ll never miss anything again!

Want it? Have it!” brings together your friends in the Brandish community. See a product you Want? Or already have? Just select either the “Want it” or “Have it” button and begin the conversation! See who already has it or who wants it! Share you wants and haves with your friends! It’s easy and fun!

“Explore” is your gateway to the most popular products and videos on the Brandish today! Simply select a category and start scrolling to discover what’s new!

Thanks again for downloading Brandish!
