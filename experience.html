THE BRANDISH EXPERIENCE

Let’s go over a few of our great features that will enhance your Brandish viewing experience: The Feed, Want it? Have it! and Explore:

  • The PLUS ICON get’s you started. It takes you to the categories and let’s you personalize the content that interests you. Select only what you really like or if you love everything, just SELECT ALL.

Now this is where the magic of Brandish begins….

  • The“Feed” is your connection to all of your selected interests. It’s where you will find amazing experiences daily, providing you with the newest and most relevant HIGH QUALITY videos. Everyday there will be new content, just for you! Brandish makes sure you’ll never miss anything again!

  • “Want it? Have it!” After watching a video, you can click “Want it or Have it” and join the Brandish Social community. Simply slide to discover who already has it or who wants it! Share you wants and haves with your friends. This feature allows you to share your interests with your friends on all of the social channels (Facebook, Snapchat, twitter and of course, Brandish) as well as give feedback, get real time reviews, make a wish list and start conversations around the products you love. It’s easy and fun!

  • “Explore” is your gateway to the all of the videos on the Brandish, unfiltered. If you see something you like, simply press the plus icon under the video and that category will be automatically added to your feed.

Thanks again for downloading Brandish!
