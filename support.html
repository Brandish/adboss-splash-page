CREATING AN ACCOUNT AND USERNAME

To get started, download the Brandish app from the iTunes App Store for iOS or from the Google Play Store for Android. Then follow the steps below to sign up for a new account.

  1. On the Welcome screen, tap 'SIGN UP.'
  2. Tap the field that says 'Email Address' and enter a valid email address. It's important to have a valid email address associated with your account. If you forget your password, your email address can be used to reset it.
  3. Tap the field that says 'Password' and enter a secure password. Your password must be at least 8 characters.
  4. Choose your username. Your username is your identity on Brandish. Make it something unique to you and remember that you cannot change your username once you have set it.
  5. Verify your phone number. This step is optional, and you can verify your phone number at any time. If you ever forget your password, we can send a password reset text to your verified phone number.
  6. Lastly, prove you're not a robot! Once you've completed this step you're ready to begin your Brandish Experience!

ADVANCED TIPS!

If your preferred email address changes, you can update and re-verify the e-mail linked to your account in Settings.

If you verify your phone number you can enable Login Verification to help make your account more secure.
