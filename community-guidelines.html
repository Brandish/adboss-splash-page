Community Guidelines

Brandish has a robust community of users that can share their wants and have’s using our Want it? Have it! feature. This allows for conversations to be started and shared around a favorite video or product. Brandish encourages this type of sharing but we would like to remind our users to be respectful to one another so that we can continue to bring you a safe and enjoyable experience while in the Brandish environment.

Keep it legal. Don’t use Brandish for any illegal activity or communications. We’re here to enjoy and discover the best products and videos on the web. Not to harass, degrade or negatively impact any of our users or brand partners.

As Brandish moves forward with new feature sets, you will have the ability to use your camera to upload your own pictures and video. For a great experience a little common sense can go a long way.

Don’t Upload:

  • Pornography
  • Nudity or sexually suggestive content involving minors (people under the age of 18)
  • Minors engaged in activities that are physically dangerous and harmful
  • Invasions of privacy
  • Threats
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Impersonation
  • Self-harm

Violating these rules may result in the removal of content, the suspension of your account and being prohibited from using Brandish in the future.

Please take these rules seriously and honor them in the spirit in which they are intended. The rules will change and evolve along with the Brandish user community. We will do our best to enforce them consistently and fairly, and ultimately we’ll try to do what we think is best in each situation, at our own discretion.

-The Brandish Team
