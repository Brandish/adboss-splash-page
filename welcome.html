Welcome to Brandish

Our Mission

We provide the full picture of what’s new from your favorite brands and help you explore your favorite categories: from advertising to the behind-the-scenes, from the voice of the makers to the voice of consumers and experts. All in one place.

Our Vision

To be the ultimate platform that explores what’s new from your favorite brands. Brandish magnifies the soul of the brand making a complete and immersive experience for our users.

Our Unique Proposition

Four layers to explore your favorite brands and new products - all in one place:

  1. Branded Communication: You know that feeling of anticipation before Super Bowl Ads? Brandish brings you that experience, personalized to your interests, every day.
  2. Additional video content: We showcase the full brand story with behind-the-scenes access, vintage advertising, branded content, how-to videos, tutorials and consumer generated videos and reviews. The full story and purpose of the brand, always in High Quality – all in one place.
  3. Brandish community::Reviews and sentiments from the users – make your voice heard! Share your want’s and have’s with Brandish community members as well as all channels of social media. Build ‘your-brand’ profile and follow other users that share the same interests.
  4. Promotions and specials: Thanks to the unique programming algorithm behind Brandish, brands are able to promote specials, discounts and offers at the exact times that they are available.

Our Audience and Our Value Add

End Users who are passionate and curious about brands and their newest products, services and more. They are skeptical of intrusive ads. They enjoy the fun of high quality, objective advertising on their own time, and they want to know more. They are smart and inquisitive. They like to research and compare.

Brand Owners, Advertisers and Media: A new integrated platform that creates a holistic brand experience for the end-consumer and enhances consumer engagement and marketing spend ROI.

New pioneer brands that deserve to be discovered, and need a new, innovative and powerful platform to help grow their brand.

Market research: competitive tracking, surveys, product tests, generational insights.
